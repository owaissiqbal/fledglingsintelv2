### What is it like to be a learner here / What does the provider do well

> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> Apprentices and students value the high levels of industry expertise that trainers
> have. Trainers use their experience to make training relevant for apprentices and
> students. Trainers skilfully plan their teaching so that apprentices can see how what
> they are learning relates to their workplace. Because of this, apprentices can apply
> their new knowledge and skills at work quickly, and markedly grow in confidence
> and skill as they become more effective employees.
>
> Apprentices and students are highly motivated and enjoy their training. They aspire
> to the high levels of professionalism which their trainers and employers demonstrate
> and promote. They confidently use the technical terminology that trainers teach
