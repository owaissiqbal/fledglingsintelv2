> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?

> Learners demonstrate positive attitudes to their learning and are enthusiastic about

>
> Learners attend their one-to-one training, their work and work placements well.

> impact on their learning. When learners fall behind, tutors provide suitable support

> Too many learners have a limited understanding of the risks of radicalisation and
> extremism and how these risks could relate to their lives and the world around
