### What is it like to be a learner here / What does the provider do well

> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> The vast majority of learners and apprentices are very satisfied with their courses.
> They feel supported, challenged and valued. They enjoy their lessons and are
> motivated to learn. Adult learners who have been out of education for some time
> benefit from activities that promote their participation without overwhelming them.
>
> Apprentices and adult learners develop their knowledge and skills well. Almost all
> apprentices stay with their employers and make a difference in their workplaces. A
> very high proportion gain salary increases and promotions. Adult learners take their
> next steps towards employment.
>
> Development coaches work closely with employers to help apprentices develop their
> professional behaviours and to practise their skills. Adult learners benefit from
> courses that leaders have developed in consultation with local stakeholders.
>
> Tutors use a range of activities, such as small group work and presentations, that
> help learners to develop their teamwork and communication skills. This boosts their
> confidence so that they can take on more challenging tasks.
>
> Tutors and coaches create a calm and supportive environment, where learners and
> apprentices feel comfortable and safe. Learners and apprentices know whom they
> should contact if they have concerns about their own or others’ well-being.
>
