### What is it like to be a learner here / What does the provider do well

> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> Learners benefit from helpful online sessions that are conducive to learning. Tutors
> set out and uphold clear expectations of apprentices in these sessions, including
> ensuring that apprentices keep their cameras and microphones on and take part in
> discussions verbally and by using the chat boxes. Apprentices who attend the

> professional discussions with each other.

> improve their communication skills. Apprentices and learners use these skills well
