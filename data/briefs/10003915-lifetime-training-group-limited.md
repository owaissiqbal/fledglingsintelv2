>
> Most apprentices enjoy studying with Lifetime because learning coaches know them
> well, encourage them, and provide effective teaching and helpful guidance

> expectations and place a strong emphasis on apprentices’ attendance and

> Apprentices develop significant new knowledge, skills and behaviours because
