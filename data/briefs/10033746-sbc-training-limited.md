### What is it like to be a learner here / What does the provider do well

> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> Apprentices are ambitious and keen to do well in their studies. They enjoy their
> learning and are proud of their achievements. Apprentices studying level 3 business
> administrator show strong professional attitudes and are confident about their future
> careers. Almost all intend to stay and progress with their current employer.
>
> Apprentices are well-prepared for their next steps. Apprentices studying level 3 lead
> adult care worker often take on additional responsibilities, such as becoming
> dementia care champions, because of their studies.
>
> In most cases, apprentices are supported well by their employers. Employers mostly
> participate in progress reviews and contribute to the planning of additional work
> activities to complement apprentices’ learning. Apprentices studying level 2
> engineering operative have additional learning on heavy gauge welding to help them
> develop useful skills they need for their work setting.
>
> Apprentices develop their confidence and character. Apprentices studying level 2
> carpentry and joinery learn the importance of interacting professionally with
> customers when completing second fixes. They learn to show care, attention and to
> be transparent in their working practices.
>
> Apprentices feel safe at the provider and at work. They learn about safeguarding at
> the start of their apprenticeship and tutors routinely revisit topics, such as
