### What is it like to be a learner here / What does the provider do well

> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> Apprentices enjoy their training. They join online lessons promptly and attend well.
> They enjoy meeting other apprentices from a diverse range of backgrounds and
> welcome the opportunities created to share clinical experiences. Apprentices are
> proud to study with DTUK. They feel valued and encouraged to succeed.
>
> Coaches create a calm, productive and professional learning environment where
> apprentices thrive. Coaches respect apprentices’ cultural backgrounds and make
> sure all apprentices are included. For example, in breakout rooms, coaches make
> sure that apprentices listen to each other’s responses when analysing case studies
> and agreeing clinical investigation outcomes. Apprentices demonstrate respect and
> consideration for each other’s opinions throughout the discussions.
>
> Apprentices quickly develop new knowledge, skills and behaviours. Healthcare
> apprentices check and remove cannulas, recognise the signs of neurological
> disorders and speak reassuringly to parents about their babies’ medication. They
> maintain accurate patient records, carry out observations of patients diligently and
> communicate effectively in multi-disciplinary teams. Apprentices quickly become
> valued members of staff.
>
> Apprentices benefit from a well-considered range of activities that enhance their
> apprenticeship. They complete data security training and attend workshops about
> managing difficult conversations and identifying signs of mental well-being. Adult
> care apprentices participate in team building activities, during which they discuss
> how to maintain good mental health and the management of stress in the
> workplace.
>
> Apprentices feel safe. They know how to report a concern should one arise. Leaders
> provide apprentices with high-quality resources to raise awareness and
> understanding of key topics such as safeguarding, radicalisation and fundamental

> apprentices’ workplaces. As a result, apprentices understand how to stay safe and
