> Learners and apprentices enjoy their learning and demonstrate positive attitudes.

> any teaching.
>
> Learners and apprentices value the good-quality teaching and support they receive

> in the workplace, such as writing code to automate the deployment of operating
> systems.
>
> Learners and apprentices make good progress. They develop substantial new
> knowledge, skills and behaviours. Apprentices apply their learning well in the
