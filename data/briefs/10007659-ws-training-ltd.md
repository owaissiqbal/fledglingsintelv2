> What does the provider need to do to improve?

### Safeguarding

> Safeguarding
>
> The arrangements for safeguarding are effective.

> Leaders responsible for safeguarding maintain effective relationships with local
> safeguarding partnerships to keep their knowledge and awareness of local threats

> Suffolk and neighbouring areas. Leaders work with police representatives to provide
