> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> Learners enjoy learning that is carefully tailored to their needs and interests. They

> the information they have about learners at the start of their programmes, and
> emerging information that transpires while they are studying, to adapt the
> curriculum and target specific new knowledge that would benefit learners.
>
> Learners value the small group sizes and welcoming learning environment that staff
> create at each setting. Staff build positive relationships with their learners that foster
> trust. This helps learners to feel safe and confident to be themselves without
> judgement.
>
