> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> Apprentices are very satisfied with their learning experiences. They enjoy good
> relationships with staff and speak highly of the support they receive. This helps

> assessments.
>
> Most apprentices develop their knowledge and skills well. They become valued team
> members in their workplaces. Most continue working for their employers when they
> conclude their apprenticeships, and many go on to study further.
>
> Apprentices benefit from the strong relationships between their employers and staff
> at Starting Off. These relationships ensure apprentices study the things that are
> most useful to them. They have opportunities to apply in the workplace the
> knowledge they develop in the classroom.
>
> Apprentices have plenty of opportunities to participate in a wide range of additional
> activities to help them build their confidence. However, relatively few take advantage
