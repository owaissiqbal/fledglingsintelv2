### Safeguarding

> Safeguarding
>
> The arrangements for safeguarding are effective.

> There are appropriate safeguarding policies and reporting processes. Safer

> newsletters. Staff also attend training with regional ‘Prevent’ duty officers.

> Apprentices and learners have an appropriate understanding of safeguarding,
> including risks in their workplaces and the locality.
