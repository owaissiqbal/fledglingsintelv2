> What is it like to be a learner with this provider?
>
> Learners and apprentices have very positive attitudes and enjoy their training. They
> attend well and are fully ready to learn at the start of each day. Learners work
> together industriously with an appropriate sense of urgency, and demonstrate highly
> professional work attitudes throughout their time with the provider. They are very
> keen to ask questions and understand more about the telecommunications industry.
>
> Apprentices quickly develop highly sought after knowledge and skills and become
> valuable employees. Almost all apprentices complete their apprenticeship and
> achieve a distinction grade.
>
> Leaders provide extensive opportunities for learners and apprentices to extend their
> knowledge, skills and behaviours beyond the curriculum. Learners and apprentices
> undertake industry-recognised accreditations, including overhead and underground

> cables and pole stepping on congested telegraph poles.
>
> Learners and apprentices quickly grow in confidence. As they progress through their
> course, they recognise that the skills they are learning are helping them to gain
> careers in the telecommunications sector. As the course progresses, learners achieve
> at least two industry accreditations per week and develop a wide range of
> engineering skills that are attractive to employers.
>
> Learners and apprentices feel safe and well supported and know who to contact
